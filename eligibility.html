Qualifications Needed for applying as a junior web developer

Full time starting working positions ·£40,000 - £50,000

Role Overview

The Team is looking for a junior developer which has the determination to join our evergrowing engineering team. You will be able to work on live client projects alongside our proffessional developers. They will be able to mentor and guide you towards a clear pathway. This role is suitable for those who have had training or undergone our web development program and are ready to apply their newley learnt skills onto real projects.

You do not need to have a multituted of years under proffessional experience, the company is more concerned with your ability to collaborate with our team, learn efficiently and being able to write logical codes.

Required Qualifications

You must have atleast TWO of the following

  • • Passed GSCE with grade 6 and above
  • • Completed a web development program (could be ours)
  • • Equivalent vocational qualification (e.g. BTEC Level 3)
  • • Self taught and able to show proof of creating websites
  • • Apprenticeship in software development or digital technologies

Personal Qualities & Desirable Skills

These qualities will help you thrive in the role

  • logistical thinking and strong mentaility when building websites
  • Good communication skills when working with a team
  • Ability to learn new skills quickly
  • Attention to detail when writing and reviewing code
  • A positive attitude and being able to handle feedback
  • Time management and ability to prioritise tasks
